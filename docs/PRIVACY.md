# WRECKCADE Privacy Notice

Effective: July 15, 2026

WRECKCADE is a free Reddit interactive game. This notice describes the data
used by the `ramageddon-game` Devvit app when someone opens or plays it.

## Data the app uses

Reddit provides the app with the current Reddit username, an opaque Reddit
account identifier, and the post/subreddit context needed to run the game. The
app processes and stores:

- run score, scrap, wreck count, best combo, level, crew, and completion result;
- personal best, streak, career totals, and daily run count;
- daily leaderboard position and crew contribution;
- one Blueprint Vote selection for the active daily challenge; and
- the player's best daily contribution to the shared Wreckpile.

The app does not request or store passwords, email addresses, real names,
payment information, precise location, private messages, or Snoovatar images.
It does not use advertising trackers or send gameplay data to an external
analytics service.

## Why the data is used

The data powers the daily challenge, personal progress, scoreboards, crew
competition, abuse limits, the shared Wreckpile, and the community Blueprint
Vote. The username associated with the play session may be displayed publicly
with leaderboard score and contribution data inside the game.

## Storage and retention

WRECKCADE stores game records in Reddit Devvit Redis for the subreddit where
the app is installed. Player profiles expire 30 days after the latest saved
run. Player-scoped daily records, leaderboard entries, vote selections, run
receipts, and run-session records expire after no more than 17 days. Shared
crew and Wreckpile totals contain aggregate game values rather than usernames.

## Deleting game data

Open the Crew Garage and press `D` twice within five seconds, or tap
**Privacy: Delete My Data** twice. The app removes the account-keyed profile,
retained daily records, leaderboard positions, votes, run receipts, and the
player's contribution from retained shared daily totals. Reddit-level account
deletion events remain governed by Reddit and the Devvit platform.

## Sharing and service providers

Game data stays within Reddit/Devvit services except when the player chooses to
share a screenshot or other information independently. WRECKCADE does not sell
personal data and does not post or comment on a player's behalf.

## Children and eligibility

Use of the app is subject to Reddit's own age and account requirements. Do not
use WRECKCADE if you are not permitted to use Reddit in your jurisdiction.

## Changes and contact

Material changes will be reflected by updating this notice and its effective
date. To ask a privacy question or report a problem, send Modmail to the
subreddit hosting the official WRECKCADE post:
<https://www.reddit.com/message/compose?to=r/ramageddon_game_dev>.

Reddit's own privacy practices are described in the
[Reddit Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy).
